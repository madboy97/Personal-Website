Mechanical Electricity Generator

Subject: Applied Physics
In this project our group created a mechanical electricity generator which can produce electricity if we provide mechanical energy to its motor.
Working:
We started to move motor's axle by a padel which was extended from motor, As we gave that padel mechanical energy it produced electrical energy due to induction. As moving a magnet through the center of the circle caused current to flow in the wire.The current stopped when he stopped moving the magnet, and it flowed in the opposite direction when he reversed the direction of the magnet. By hand given mechanical energy we were able to give enough energy that can light a small 3v bulb
Future:
This project can be used in large scale in windy area where we can convert mechanical energy to electrical energy and it can also be used in water as through water waves we can produce electricity easily.
